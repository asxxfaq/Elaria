Ordering & Payment

To place an order, simply browse our collections, select the item and quantity you desire, click "Add to Cart," and proceed to checkout. You can checkout as a guest or create an account for faster future purchases.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and Google Pay. We also offer interest-free payment plans through [Mention any third-party payment partner like Klarna or Afterpay].

We process orders very quickly. If you need to modify or cancel, please contact us **within 2 hours** of placing the order. Once the order enters the packaging phase, we cannot guarantee changes.

Shipping & Delivery

Shipping times vary by location and shipping method selected: Standard shipping takes **5-7 business days** (free for orders over $150). Express shipping takes **2-3 business days** and costs $15. Custom or personalized items require an additional 5 business days for creation.

Once your order ships, you will receive an email confirmation containing a tracking number and a link to the carrier's website. You can also view your tracking details by logging into your Elaria account.

Returns & Exchanges

We offer a **30-day return window** from the date of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Items must be unworn, undamaged, and returned in the original packaging with all tags attached. Personalized items are final sale.
